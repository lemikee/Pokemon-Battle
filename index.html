Welcome to Pokemon Battle!

Welcome Pokemon Trainer! Your adventure begins now!

About: Pokemon Battle! is a turn based strategy game that recreates the fun you had playing Pokemon growing up! Use your knowledge of type weakness and battle mechanics to win.

Instructions: Each button corresponds to an attack of a specifc type. Click on a button to make a move. Your opponent will attack two seconds after you. The game will announce a play by play of the battle including the effectiveness of each attack!
